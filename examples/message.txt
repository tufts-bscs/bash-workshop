Send a message over halligan!

1. Figure out who is on your server
2. Pick your target
3. Type the following: cowsay "<Message>" | write <username>. 
   Replace <message> with your message and <username> with 
   someones UTLN.
4. Press enter and you've sent a cow message!!
5. Try another character. Type the following: cowsay -f <char name> "<Message>" | write <username>
